## ðŸ§  Core Mental Models

### Judo Strategy
*Use competitor's momentum against them*

Their size becomes bureaucracy. Their focus becomes blindness. Their commitments become shackles.

### The Cobra Effect
*Competitor solutions to your problems may backfire*

Create situations where their logical response creates worse problems.

### Death by a Thousand Cuts vs. Frontal Assassination
*Choose your warfare style based on resource differential*

Small players win through rapid iteration and attrition. Sometimes one decisive blow is better than gradual erosion.
